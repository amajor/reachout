DAY TWELVE: with all your heart

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight."

Proverbs 3:5-6

Read

Proverbs 3

Reflect

This passage is important because it tells us again what God should mean to us, that we need to trust him with everything we have. We are his. God is always there for us, and I can never repay him for all the ways that he has blessed me! This includes the blessing of our church family. As a young family at North Holland we were welcomed. North Holland was always a very important part of our lives and was always there for us and with us. We love North Holland, and we trust God as we look to the future!

- Ken Assink
