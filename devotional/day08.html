DAY EIGHT: faith is...

"Now faith is confidence in what we hope for and assurance about what we do not see."

Hebrews 11:1

Read

Hebrews 11:1-3

Reflect

(please read the bold aloud)

God, You charged us to walk in faith: to be assured of what we hope for and certain of what we do not see.

We confess that we are not always assured or certain of Your presence in our lives, our families, our workplaces and schools.

Give us the courage to recognize our curiosity, wanderings, and doubts,

That we may learn to fully rely on You in times of confusion and in times of clarity.

In the name of Christ our Light, Amen.

Reflect

In your journal or a blank sheet of paper, draw or write your thoughts.
