DAY FIFTEEN: one in heart and mind

"All the believers were one in heart and mind. No one claimed that any of their possessions was their own, but they shared everything they had."

Acts 4:32

Read

Acts 4:32-5:10

Reflect

(please read the bold aloud)

God, You call us to live in unity with one another as Your people, sharing our hearts and our possessions.

We confess that we are quick to give in times of abundance and slow to sacrifice what we hold close.

Convict us, God, to give what is precious,

because You call us to trust You with our deepest selves.

In the name of Christ our Light, Amen.

Reflect

In your journal or a blank sheet of paper, draw or write your thoughts.
