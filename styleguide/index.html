Headers

  • font: "a song for jennifer"
  • color:
    • LIGHT background
      • if printed in color: Navy
      • if printed in black&white: Black
    • DARK background
      • White

Headers should use the font "a song for jennifer" and should be in NAVY (if printed in color) or BLACK when over a light background or WHITE when over a dark background.

Header text should use all uppercase letters.
